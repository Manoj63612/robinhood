# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The Car Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze and visualize car market data. It helps users understand trends in pricing, performance, and distribution of different car types.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* To analyze car sales data effectively
* To identify popular car brands
* To study price trends over the years
* To understand the relationship between performance and cost
* To provide interactive filtering using slicers

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Dataset (Car Sales Data – CSV format)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. KPI Cards

* Total Cars
* Average MSRP
* Average MPG
* Average Year

### 2. Charts Used

#### 📊 Bar Chart

Displays number of cars by brand (Make) to identify popular manufacturers.

#### 📊 Column Chart

Shows average car price (MSRP) over the years to analyze price trends.

#### 🍩 Donut Chart

Represents distribution of vehicle types such as SUV, Sedan, etc.

#### 📉 Scatter Plot

Shows relationship between fuel efficiency (MPG) and price (MSRP), with engine power as size.

---

### 3. Data Views

#### 📋 Table

Displays detailed car information such as Make, Model, Year, and Price.

#### 📊 Matrix

Summarizes average price by brand and year for comparison.

---

### 4. Slicers (Filters)

* Make
* Year
* Fuel Type
* Transmission Type

These slicers allow users to interactively filter and explore the data.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* SUV vehicles dominate the dataset
* Higher engine power generally leads to higher price
* Car prices have increased over recent years
* Some brands have significantly higher average prices

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The Car Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the automobile dataset. It enables users to gain insights into pricing, performance, and distribution trends through interactive visualizations.
